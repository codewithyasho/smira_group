About Smira Group

Smira Group is a professionally managed proprietorship firm based in Narhe, Pune, specializing in comprehensive HR, payroll, compliance, and business support services.

We are committed to helping organizations streamline their workforce management and statutory compliance processes with efficient and reliable solutions.

Our Mission

To support businesses with dependable HR, payroll, compliance, and documentation services that improve operational efficiency, ensure statutory compliance, and contribute to organizational growth.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading HR and compliance service provider by delivering innovative, transparent, and client-focused workforce management solutions.
