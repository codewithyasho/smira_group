[About Us]
Smira Group is a professionally managed proprietorship firm based in Narhe, Pune, specializing in comprehensive HR, payroll, compliance, and business support services. To become a trusted and leading HR and compliance service provider by delivering innovative, transparent, and client-focused workforce management solutions.
